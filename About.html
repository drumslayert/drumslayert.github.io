Resume

My name is Terry Calhoun. Coding started as a hobby for me. :) A good friend introduced me to WordPress about 3 years ago and soon I began building websites for friends. I began attending conferences each year, and it didn't take long to realize I wanted to turn this new-found passion into a career.

In effort to educate myself further, I just spent three months immersed in a Front-End Engineering course at The Iron Yard in Durham. I put in 80-90 hours per week working independently, in pairs and on teams. Our lectures & projects consisted of Javascript, jQuery, Angular, Node, Ajax, CSS, SASS, HTML5, API's and of course the command-line. We used the Agile method utilizing github & morning stand-ups everyday and for every project. During our final project, we also used Trello to organize our milestones for each iteration and successfully built out a complete web application from scratch in just a 2&1/2 week sprint.

Today, I'm currently working as a Contract Developer with a company called Fan Base located in Raleigh NC.  I build out new websites in Wordpress, as well as update, add new content or repair broken sites for new and existing clients.  I also work with a team of great people from Coalmarch Tech Productions. We collaborate together using basecamp and on projects with them I use github, ftp and pantheon.

I can honestly say, as scary as it has been making such a big career shift with a family of 4, having two very young girls, moving across several states. I truly have enjoyed every minute of it so far :)  It's forced me to step out of my comfort zones.  One thing that I love about technology is, it's always changing. And one thing that I love that will NEVER change, is helping people and the joy that it brings me.  I want to be a part of success stories.  I want to design, develop and create real solutions to real problems, making technology work in out favor.
